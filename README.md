# 🍪 Cookie Clicker

A browser-based cookie clicker game built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## Features
- Click the cookie to earn cookies
- Buy upgrades to increase cookies per click
- Number formatting (1,000 / 1M / 1B)
- Reset button to start over

## Getting Started
Just visit `[Play Cookie Clicker!](https://caliberdev-prog.github.io/Cookie-Clicker/)` in your browser.

## How to Play
1. Click the 🍪 to earn cookies
2. Spend cookies on upgrades to earn more per click
3. See how many cookies you can rack up!

## Upgrades
| Upgrade | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| ⚡ Double Click Power | 10 | +1 per click |
| 🔥 Hot Oven | 100 | +5 per click |
| ✨ Magic Dough | 500 | +20 per click |
| 🤖 Robot Baker | 2,000 | +100 per click |
| 🌟 Cookie God | 10,000 | +500 per click |
